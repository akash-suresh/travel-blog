> To Isha, the person who made me fill the form!

An uneventful summer. An amazing opportunity. A traveler.

Each one of us dream of flying. If only I had wings you hear more often than not. This is the story of my first fly over the seas. This is the tale of my visit to the kingdom of Ravana. This is the story of my visit to Sri Lanka.

Finally the day dawned. I had gone around telling everyone about the upcoming adventure. Packing all the stuff I would need, I headed towards the airport. I had to catch a flight from Bengaluru to Chennai and onwards to Colombo. 

**Fun fact** - SpiceJet names each of their planes uniquely with the names of spices. Also, SpiceJet was the cheapest flight to Lanka.

We landed in Colombo and caught a taxi to Hotel Galadari. This is one of those 5/5 hotels. Recommend this to everyone heading to Colombo. The view from my room was just wow. You could see the blue waters of the Indian Ocean. Quickly gobbling up breakfast, I skipped down the stairs (It was in a lift, the room was on the 7th floor. But yeah, better narration). The roads were super clean and well maintained, by Indian standards. 


Excited at the prospect of being on foreign lands, I set out to wander about the streets. I am going to let my photos tell you the rest of the story.
